About Me


I graduated with a B.A. in Human Development from Washington State University. Since graduating in 2015, I've worked 2 years in HR type roles and 2 years in Education as a Before and After School Program Manager for the Issaquah School District. As a kid, I used to build website layouts with HTML and CSS as a preferred hobby but never pursued it as a career until now.

After taking one free Javascript class online, it definitely re-kindled my flame for pursuing a career as a Web Developer. Coding challenges me in ways I've never been pushed before and there is no better feeling than solving those bumps in the roads that take hours maybe days to debug.

In my freetime, my passion is to DJ. I've been DJing for a couple of years now and am slowly securing gigs around the Greater Seattle area night life.
